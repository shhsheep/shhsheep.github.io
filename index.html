A Game

Prologue

Bushy, a red squirrel, is taking a science class the following year. Her summer assignment is to write a short, eye-opening report that introduces a living thing in her neighborhood. The topic she decided to write about—algae—is based on the green mass in a nearby pond. From the assignment, she discovers their ecological importance and their many applications in daily life. Excited to share her research, Bushy opens her room to interested squirrels.

Controls

click to interact; spacebar to continue dialogue
